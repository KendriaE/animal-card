American Pitbull Terrier

Pit Bulls were popular mascots in early 20th Century America, often appearing on Army recruitment posters and other advertisements.

  • Scientific Name American Pitbull
  • Average Length 3 feet
  • Average Lifespan 10 years
  • Habitat Adaptable to most climates

The truth about pit bulls is simple: They’re sweet, smart, hilarious, loyal companions. They really, really love people. They love to snuggle, cuddle, roll over for belly rubs, crawl in your lap if you’ll allow it, and stay as close to you as possible all day long.
